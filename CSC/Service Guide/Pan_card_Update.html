PAN Card Update

₹250

Processing time: 7–10 working days  · 

What This Service Does

Updates or corrects the details associated with your existing PAN card, including name, date of birth, father’s name, and address. This ensures your PAN information remains accurate for income tax filing, banking, and other financial transactions.

Documents Required

  • Existing PAN Card (copy)
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, Passport, Driving License, or Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Aadhaar Card, Utility Bill, Bank Statement, or Passport)
  • Proof of Date of Birth (Birth Certificate, Matric Certificate, or Passport)
  • Recent passport-size photograph
