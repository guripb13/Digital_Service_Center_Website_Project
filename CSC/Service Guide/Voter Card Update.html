Voter Card Update

₹150

Processing time: 7–10 working days  · 

What This Service Does

Updates the details linked to an existing Voter ID card — corrections to name, date of birth, address, and other personal information. This service ensures that your voter record remains accurate and up to date for electoral purposes.

Documents Required

  • Original Voter ID card (if available)
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, or Driving License)
  • Proof of Address (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport)
  • Passport-size photograph(required if want to update photograph)
