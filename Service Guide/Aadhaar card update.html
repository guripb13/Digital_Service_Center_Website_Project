Aadhaar Card Update

₹80

Processing time: 7–10 working days  · 

What This Service Does

Updates the details linked to an existing Aadhaar number — corrections to name, date of birth, gender, and address, as well as biometric updates such as fingerprints, iris scan, and photograph. The Aadhaar number itself never changes; only the information recorded against it is corrected.

Documents Required

  • Original Aadhaar card
  • One proof of identity (passport, PAN card, or voter ID)
  • One proof of address if the address is being updated
